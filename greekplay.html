The Seven Plays in English Verse by Sophocles

OED. Daughter, what must I think, or do? ANT. My sire! We must conform us to the people's will, Yielding ere they compel. OED. Give me thy hand. ANT. Thou hast it. OED. --Strangers, let me not Be wronged, when I have trusted you And come from where I stood! CH. Assure thee, from this seat No man shall drag thee off against thy will. OED. Farther? CH. Advance thy foot. OED. Yet more? CH. Assist him onward Maiden, thou hast thy sight.
